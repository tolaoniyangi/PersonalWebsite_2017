What does it mean for a theatre to straddle the threshold between a natural landscape and an urban condition?

This redevelopment and expansion of a theatre in Greenpoint was done primarily from a technical perspective. The site context gives credence to the concept to integrate the sloping terrain of the park, which descends into the river, with the hard edge and scale of the warehouse buildings towards the north. Decisions such as louvers controlling light entering the building depending on the orientation, green walls on the interior and throughout the accessible sloping roof, and an operable glass partition between the theatre and the park opposite it take into account a relationship between people, nature, and art.
