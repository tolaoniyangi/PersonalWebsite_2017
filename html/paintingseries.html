Can transformations in life be captured through painting?

This series of paintings stems from the idea that change is constant. The first represents breaking out of a rigid grid. The second portrays a shift towards dynamism and change through the painting's color and energy. The third shows a complete break from the grid and a movement towards vibrancy, fun, and ultimately, freedom.

Spring 2016. Painting 1. Taught by Victoria Roth.
