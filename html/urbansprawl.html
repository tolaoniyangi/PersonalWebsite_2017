Can we create a model to predict the expansion of cities?

In this machine learning project, a model that could predict urban sprawl was trained by being fed historical images of cities using Python. The model learnt to recognize built and unbuilt areas and identify them with different colors, and thus was able to predict future sprawl.

Fall 2016. Data Mining the City - taught by Danil Nagy. In collaboration with Marwah Garib, Vrinda Sharma and Kun Qian.
