How can we measure a neighborhood’s “vibe” and communicate it to a certain demographic?

This video mapping project is tailored to prospective college students through a fun graphic sensibility, lighthearted questions and a peppy musical score. It analyzes how suitable Brooklyn's Flatbush Avenue between Tillary and Fulton streets is for an ideal college neighborhood using a checklist of important qualities prospective students look for in their future neighborhoods. Data used includes field work and information from various city and national agencies.

Fall 2015. Datascapes and the Informal City - taught by Leah Meisterlin. In collaboration with Joud Al Shdaifat and Kamay Jin.
